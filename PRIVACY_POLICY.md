## Introduction

ChatterFix ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our CMMS platform.

Please read this policy carefully. By using ChatterFix, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Name and email address
- Company/organization name
- Job title and role
- Password (encrypted)
- Profile preferences

**Business Data**
- Work orders and maintenance records
- Asset information and maintenance history
- Inventory and parts data
- Vendor information
- Team member details
- Training records and certifications

**Communications**
- Support requests and feedback
- Survey responses
- Email correspondence

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data**
- Features and pages accessed
- Actions taken within the platform
- Time and duration of use
- Error logs and performance data

**Device Information**
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
- Screen resolution

**Location Data**
- IP address
- General geographic location (city/region level)
- For mobile: GPS data (only with your permission)

**Cookies and Tracking**
- Session cookies for authentication
- Preference cookies for settings
- Analytics cookies for usage patterns

### 3. AI and Voice Data

**Voice Commands**
- Audio recordings (processed, not stored permanently)
- Transcribed text from voice commands
- Voice command history for context

**AI Interactions**
- Questions asked to AI assistant
- AI responses provided
- Feedback on AI accuracy

## Data Security

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security:

- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
- **Authentication:** Firebase Auth with secure session management
- **Access Control:** Role-based permissions, least privilege principle
- **Infrastructure:** Google Cloud Platform with SOC 2 compliance
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring and alerting
- **Backups:** Automated encrypted backups with disaster recovery

### Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:
- Maintaining strong passwords
- Controlling access to your account
- Logging out from shared devices
- Reporting suspected breaches

## Data Retention

### Retention Periods

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account data | Duration of account + 30 days |
| Business data | Duration of account + 30 days |
| Usage logs | 90 days |
| AI interactions | 30 days (or per preference) |
| Security logs | 1 year |
| Backup data | 90 days after deletion |

### Upon Account Deletion

When you delete your account:
1. Active data deleted within 30 days
2. Backup copies purged within 90 days
3. Aggregate, anonymized data may be retained
4. Legal holds may extend retention
